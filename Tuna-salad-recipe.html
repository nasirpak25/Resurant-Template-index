Tuna Salad with Quinao

Ingredients

    1 can of Tuna (80g)

    100g Quinoa

    Mixed Salad (200g)

    20g Olive oil

Recipe

    Crack the Eggs into bowl and Whisk them until well beaten.

    Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat and add the butter.

    Once the butter has melted, pour the beaten eggs into pan.

    Lets the eggs sit for a few seconds to start cooking, the gently stir them with a spatula.

    Continue stirring the eggs occasionally untill they are mostly cooked but still slightly runny.

    Remove the pan from heat and let the residual heat finish cookung the eggs to your desired consistency.
